FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is GENOIN HORIZON ECOSYSTEM?
GENOIN Horizon is a unified intelligent digital platform that brings together AI, cloud infrastructure, smart commerce, identity management, payment gateways, developer APIs, analytics, and automation into a single, seamlessly integrated ecosystem — enabling businesses of all sizes to build, deploy, and scale digital products globally.
How does GENOIN ensure enterprise-grade security?
Security is built into every layer of GENOIN Horizon. We maintain SOC2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA compliance. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Our zero-trust architecture, biometric identity, and continuous security monitoring ensure your data is always protected.
What are the pricing models available?
GENOIN offers flexible pricing: a free Starter tier for developers, a Growth plan for scaling startups, and Enterprise plans with custom pricing for large organizations. You only pay for what you use — no hidden fees. Contact our sales team for volume discounts and custom enterprise packages.
Can I customize and extend the platform?
Absolutely. GENOIN Horizon is fully extensible. Use our REST, GraphQL, and WebSocket APIs, 20+ language SDKs, and our App Marketplace with 5,000+ integrations. You can build custom modules, white-label the entire platform, and integrate with any existing system via our universal connector framework.
What support options are available?
We offer 24/7 technical support across all plans, with dedicated account managers and SLA-backed response times for Enterprise customers. Developer support includes community forums, interactive docs, a sandbox environment, and real-time chat with our engineering team.
Which cloud providers does GENOIN support?
GENOIN Horizon is cloud-agnostic. It runs natively on AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, and our own sovereign cloud infrastructure. You can deploy to any combination of providers simultaneously, or use our fully managed cloud for the simplest experience.
How does the AI engine work?
The GENOIN AI Engine is a multimodal inference platform supporting LLMs, computer vision, NLP, and custom ML models. You can use pre-built AI modules or bring your own models. All inference runs on our GPU clusters with less than 10ms latency, and you can fine-tune models on your own data with a single API call.
Is there a free trial or developer sandbox?
Yes. Every developer gets a free sandbox with full access to all GENOIN APIs, including AI inference, payments (test mode), cloud storage, and identity. No credit card required. The free Starter tier includes generous usage limits to take you from idea to MVP without any cost.
